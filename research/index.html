Research Interests

My primary research involves the rigorous analysis of differential equations, focusing on qualitative properties such as existence, uniqueness, regularity, and spatial behavior of solutions.

  • Nonlinear elliptic partial differential equations
  • Fully nonlinear elliptic operators
  • Viscosity solutions
  • Singular nonlinearities
  • Principal eigenvalue problems
  • Shape optimization
  • Pucci extremal operators
  • Mathematical biology and optimal control

Current Focus

Currently, I am investigating the boundary behavior of solutions to singular equations involving fully nonlinear operators. A key aspect of this work includes establishing comparison principles and understanding the role of principal eigenvalues in domains with specific geometric properties.
