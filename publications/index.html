Publications and Preprints

My research publications are mainly in nonlinear elliptic and parabolic partial differential equations, fully nonlinear operators, Pucci extremal operators, singular nonlinearities, bifurcation and multiplicity theory, optimal control, and shape optimization.

A complete and updated list is also available in my CV and academic profiles.
