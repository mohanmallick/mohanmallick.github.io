Biography

Welcome! I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT), Nagpur.

My academic work centers on studying both pure and applied aspects of differential equations, developing qualitative properties of solutions, and exploring their applications in mathematical models.

Research Overview

My research interests lie broadly in the analysis of nonlinear elliptic partial differential equations, fully nonlinear elliptic operators, viscosity solutions, singular nonlinearities, principal eigenvalue problems, shape optimization, Pucci extremal operators, and mathematical biology/optimal control.

For a more detailed overview of my ongoing projects and recent works, please visit the Research and Publications pages.
